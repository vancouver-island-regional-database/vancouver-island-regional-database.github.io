Vancouver Island Regional Database

The Vancouver Island Regional Database is an open-access portal of public sector and local governance records. The catalogue is organized by theme to promote public access, use, integration, and preservation across overlapping jurisdictions in the Mid-Island area.

Available Datasets

Water Systems | Mid-Island

Records regarding management and operation of sewer systems, water systems, protected watersheds, wastewater treatment, and the coastline.
